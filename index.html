This is a naming system based on the ghananaian culture commonly refferred to as "Day Name".

The naming system is a culture invented by the ghananians. It is a system where children were given a name according to the day of the week of their birth. The naming tradition was diversly used through ghana until it gain popularity and transversed through-out West-Africa and other Africa diaspora.
